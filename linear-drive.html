Linear Drive Assembly

LD9000 · 350 mm screw · TR12×3 – 7e

A complete lead-screw linear actuator — modeled, detailed, and documented as a full drawing package: six custom machined parts, standard catalog hardware, an exploded view with bill of materials, and fully dimensioned sheets to ANSI drafting standards.

Assembly

The carriage plate rides twin guide rails on four pillow blocks while a TR12×3 stainless lead screw, supported in dry-running sleeve bearings at each end block, drives a UHMW nut captured in the carriage. Section and detail views document the bearing fit and set-screw retention.

Machined end blocks that anchor both ends of the drive. Each block carries a counterbored Ø14 bearing pocket for the flanged sleeve bearings, tapped M5×0.8 holes for the rail set screws, and Ø10 rail seats with relief slots.

The moving platform of the assembly. Eight Ø6.6 mounting holes (four counterbored) tie the plate to the pillow blocks, a Ø15 clearance bore passes the lead screw nut boss, and 2.0 × 45° chamfered feet clear the rail profile.
